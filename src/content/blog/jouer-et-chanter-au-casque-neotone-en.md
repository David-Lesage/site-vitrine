The [Neotone](/le-neotone), an electronic handpan, lets you play and accompany your singing **on headphones** — late at night, without waking anyone. Where an acoustic handpan sounds through the whole house, the Neotone keeps the sound for you alone. It's electronic's quietest and most liberating edge.

## The wall of acoustic volume

An acoustic handpan is beautiful — and loud. It resonates, it carries, it fills a room and spills through the walls. Wonderful by day, outdoors, when sharing. Much trickier in the evening, in a flat, with children asleep or neighbours close by. How many times does the urge to play run into "not now, it'll disturb everyone"?

That wall stops the momentum dead. Yet it's often in the evening, once quiet returns, that the urge comes to settle in, play a soft loop, sing a little. The ideal moment for music is also the worst for volume.

## Headphones change everything

The Neotone tears down that wall. Plug in headphones, and **you play and sing in total silence for the rest of the house**. The music exists fully in your ears, and nowhere else. No more trade-off between your urge to play and other people's sleep.

This is especially precious for **accompanying your own singing**. Singing takes feeling free, safe from judgement — and at night on headphones, you're alone with your voice and your instrument. You can slip up, start over, search for your key without anyone hearing. That intimacy melts away the shyness that blocks so many people when it comes to singing.

### The method travels with the instrument

The Neotone isn't just a silent handpan: it carries the whole [Handpan Studio](/handpan-studio) approach. The [Logic Mode](/blog/handpan-emotions-degres) with its seven coloured chords, the [vocal layouts](/blog/layouts-vocaux-handpan) to settle your voice, transposition to sing in your key — it's all there, screen and instrument speaking the same language. Your late-night singing-accompaniment session has exactly the same tools as by day.

### The right moment, finally available

Electronic doesn't replace the magic of acoustic: it extends it to moments that were closed to it. At night, on the move, in a hotel room, next to a sleeping baby — the Neotone makes playing possible where acoustic must stay silent. And often, it's those stolen moments that make the finest sessions.
