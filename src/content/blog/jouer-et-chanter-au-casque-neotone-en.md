The [Neotone](/le-neotone), an electronic handpan, plays through **headphones** — late at night, in a flat, on the road, disturbing no one. Where an acoustic handpan rings through the whole house, the Neotone keeps the sound for you alone. And it isn't just about discretion: with headphones, you get an **immersive experience** that acoustic simply cannot offer.

## The acoustic volume wall

An acoustic handpan is magnificent — and loud. It resonates, it carries, it fills a room and spills through the walls. Wonderful in the daytime, outdoors, in sharing. Much trickier in the evening, in a flat, with household members asleep or sensitive neighbours. How many times does the urge to play run into "not now, it'll disturb people"?

That wall stops the impulse dead. Yet it's often in the evening, once calm has returned, that you feel like settling down and playing a gentle loop. The ideal moment for music is also the worst one for volume.

## Headphones knock that wall down

Plug headphones into the Neotone's dedicated output, and **you play in total silence for the rest of the house**. The music fully exists in your ears, and nowhere else. No more trade-off between your urge to play and other people's peace — at night, in a hotel room, in a van, anywhere.

## An immersion acoustic playing doesn't know

That's the surprise of headphone playing: it isn't a degraded mode, it's a **different experience**.

- **Spatialised stereo sound**: the notes spread through the space around your head, each timbre in its place. You are literally *inside* the instrument.
- **Total purity**: no room noise, no stray reverberation — just the sound of the handpan, captured at the source, in a definition that even an excellent speaker doesn't render this closely.
- **Rare focus**: cut off from sound distractions, you hear every detail of your playing — the nuances, the fingering, the silences. Many players progress faster with headphones for that very reason.

It's the intimate counterpart of [the speaker placement I recommend](/blog/etre-bien-dans-le-son-neotone): the speaker behind you to share, the headphones to immerse yourself.

### The method travels with the instrument

The Neotone isn't just a silent handpan: it carries the whole [Handpan Studio](/handpan-studio) approach. The [Logic Mode](/blog/handpan-emotions-degres) and its seven coloured chords, scale creation, transposition — everything is there, screen and instrument speaking the same language. Your night session has exactly the same tools as daytime.

### The right moment, finally available

Electronic doesn't replace the magic of acoustic: it extends it to moments that were closed to it. At night, on the road, in a hotel room — the Neotone makes playing possible where acoustic must stay silent. And often, those stolen moments make the most beautiful sessions.
