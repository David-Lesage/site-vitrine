An electronic handpan like the **Neotone** gives you the feel and gesture of an acoustic handpan, but with the freedom of digital: you play with headphones at midnight without waking anyone, you switch scales in a second, you add effects, you record in studio quality and you connect it to your computer over MIDI. One instrument, a world of possibilities.

## Play at any hour, with headphones

The first obstacle with an acoustic handpan is volume: playing late without disturbing anyone is impossible. The Neotone solves that instantly. Plug in headphones and play at 3 a.m. just like mid-afternoon. For many players, this single detail is what turns "I rarely play" into "I play every day".

## Endless scales in a single instrument

An acoustic handpan is tuned once and for all: one scale, one key. The Neotone carries an **atlas of world scales** and lets you move between them instantly. Want a meditative minor scale in the morning and a bright one at night? Two gestures are enough. You can even create and load your own scales.

### Effects, recording and studio quality

- **Built-in effects**: reverb, delay and textures to shape your sound without external gear.
- **Direct recording**: capture your ideas in studio quality, no microphone or complex processing.
- **Consistent sound**: no more notes drifting out of tune over time or temperature.

### MIDI: your handpan becomes a controller

Over **MIDI**, the Neotone drives any music software. It becomes an expressive controller for composing, layering sounds or playing virtual instruments — all while keeping the natural gesture of the handpan.

## Who is the Neotone for?

- **The beginner** who wants to learn without buying several pans in different keys.
- **The apartment player** who needs headphones.
- **The creator** who wants to record, add effects and connect the instrument to their workstation.
- **The curious** who wants to explore dozens of scales before knowing which they prefer.

Paired with the visual colour method, the instrument becomes even more intuitive: discover [handpan by colours (ChromaKeys)](/en/blog/handpan-par-les-couleurs) to understand how the screen and the instrument speak the same language.

The electronic handpan takes nothing away from the magic of acoustic: it simply opens one more door — at any hour and in every scale.
